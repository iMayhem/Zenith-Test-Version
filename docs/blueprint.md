# **App Name**: Zenith Hub

## Core Features:

- Virtual Environment Selection: Allow users to select from a variety of high-definition video backgrounds to simulate different workspaces.
- Custom Ambient Soundscapes: Enable users to create personalized soundscapes by mixing various ambient sounds (e.g., rain, cafe, forest).
- Pomodoro Timer: Integrate a Pomodoro timer with customizable work and break intervals.
- Configurable Digital Clock: Display a customizable digital clock with options for different time formats and themes.
- Workspace Suggestion Tool: Generative AI tool to recommend optimal workspace environment (video background and soundscape mix) based on user-specified task, mood, and focus level.
- Study Together Place: A virtual place where lots of students can come and sit together to study and their time gets counted live with leaderboard

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft, muted blue (#6FA8DC) to promote calmness and focus.
- Background color: Light, desaturated blue-green (#DEEAD7) to evoke a serene atmosphere.
- Accent color: Warm yellow-orange (#FFB74D) to mimic warm accent lighting and highlight important elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif, to offer a clean, modern look.
- Use minimalist icons with rounded corners for a soft, approachable feel.
- Employ a clean and spacious layout with ample whitespace to reduce distractions and enhance focus.
- Incorporate subtle, calming animations and transitions (e.g., cross-fading video backgrounds) to create a smooth, immersive experience.
